Dive into a whole new world with snorkeling in Taniti, where vibrant coral reefs and colorful marine life await just beneath the surface. Swim alongside tropical fish in crystal-clear waters and experience the island’s beauty up close in a calm, unforgettable way.

About Taniti's Attractions

Most tourists spend most of their time in Taniti City, which boasts native architecture and nearby white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay. Many of the activities mentioned above are located in Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.
